Hierarchy Scope

The hierarchy scope identifies where the exchanged information fits within the role based equipment hierarchy. It defines the scope of the exchanged information, such as site area or area for which the information is relevant. The hierarchy scope also identifies the associated instance in the role based equipment hierarchy.

Equipment ID {{option.hierarchyScope.equipmentLevel}} - {{option.id}} A unique identification of an equipment element the exhcanged information is relevant. Equipment Level {{option}} Identification of the equipment level if the equipment element is defined.
